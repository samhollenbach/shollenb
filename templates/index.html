Sam Hollenbach

Student / Software Developer

I started programming in 2010 at age 13, making mods for the video game “Minecraft”. This is what first got me into programming and since then I never looked back. Lately I have been writing extensively in Python, and am working on a full stack mobile application and website for my baseball team. I have completed coursework in database management, internet computing, and artificial intelligence, and have used these concepts in my independent work.

Macalester College Varisty Baseball Headshot

Most importantly, after nearly 9 years of programming I pick up new theories and languages much more quickly than I did even a few years ago. I am currently teaching myself C++ and Rust, along with learning new web frameworks to support more versatile web development. Check out the Projects and Experience tabs to see examples of my past work.
